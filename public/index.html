About Amy

Amy Zinger believes everyone and everything has a story to tell. As a creative director and writer, she's told stories for some of the world's most famous brands, and as a soprano, she's brought some of opera's most well-known characters to life—including the iconic Queen of the Night. Her other interests include coding (badly), photography (passably), cooking (well), and playing chess (also badly). Amy lives in Dallas, Texas, with her husband, daughter, and three ridiculous dogs. She is never bored.
